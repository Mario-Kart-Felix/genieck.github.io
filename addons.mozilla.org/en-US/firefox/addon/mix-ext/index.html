About this extension
This add-on is a beta version. Have feedback you'd like to share? Submit it here, we'd love to hear from you!

Rediscover the web without the noise.

Mix is a new personalized discovery platform that unlocks more of the internet you care about.

★ Mix Explore Button
The Mix Explore button allows you to uncover the internet's hidden gems by recommending you a personalized, uninterrupted stream of content based on the things you like - explore the internet one page at a time with a single click.

★ Personalized recommendations
With each click, Mix learns what you're passionate about and queues up pages based on your interests, the collections you're subscribed to, and the people you follow. We left noisy comments and black box algorithms behind to surface the things you want to see using a combination of machine learning, editor picks, and best-in-class publishers.

★ Save anything from anywhere
Easily save your favorite pages to your profile or collections using the + button to find later, share with friends, and more. Click the plus button twice to organize pages into lists called collections.

★ Share and direct message
Share interesting pages and have conversations with a friend using the share button from the toolbar.

★ Profile & Activity stream
Access your latest discoveries you’ve mixed to your profile and/or see what other people have added using the activity button right from the toolbar.

Browse corners of the internet your other social feeds may skip over, one page at a time, with the Mix browser button.

We’re still in beta and would love to hear from you. Feel free to email us directly at feedback@mix.com or submit feedback on this for here: https://community.mix.com/blog/2019/8/extension-beta-submit-your-feedback
