AWS Storage Competency Partners

Deploy seamless storage solutions across your cloud and on-premises environments

AWS Storage Competency Partners are comprised of APN Consulting and Technology Partners that provide industry-leading consulting and technology services for a variety of use cases, including Backup & Restore operations to, from, and within the AWS environment; primary storage using IP File or Block protocols and object storage; active and passive data archiving capabilities; and business continuity/disaster recovery (BCDR) solutions.

Find AWS Storage Competency Partners »

AWS Storage Competency Categories

APN Consulting Partners offer consulting services that provide implementation capabilities in one or more core storage use cases. To search for an APN Consulting Partner, click the “Consulting” button under the “Partner Type” filter below.

APN Technology Partners have demonstrated success helping customers evaluate and use the techniques and technologies of effectively moving, using, and protecting data to and on the AWS cloud. Their offerings have been vetted and validated for demonstrated technical proficiency and proven customer success in one or more of the specialized APN Technology Partner Categories listed below.

Archive

Solutions that leverage Amazon S3 Glacier for durable and cost-effective long-term data backup.

Backup & Restore

Backup and restore solutions protect data from physical or logical errors, such as system failure, application error, or accidental deletion.

Business Continuity/Disaster Recovery

Solutions that utilize AWS to enable recovery strategies focused on recovery time and recovery point objective requirements.

Primary Storage

Solutions that leverage file, block, object, and streamed data formats as an extension to on-premises storage.
