It is a sad fact that Wikidot.Com has been under increasing attack by spammers. Many wiki admins have been forced to lock pages or restrict access to members only to fight spam - which unfortunately makes it more difficult for honest users to contribute.

You can report either a user or a site if it breaks the Wikidot.com Terms of Service. Please double-check the terms before you report anyone.

Also made a search for the user or site name - often the page was created before!
