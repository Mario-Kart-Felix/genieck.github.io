45 Cool Snaps Show Lifestyle of American Teens in the Late 1980s

 April 14, 2020     1980s, children & youth, fashion & clothing, life & culture, portraits   
The 1980s is the period of multi-style fashion, especially for young people in the United States. From hairstyles to clothes, everything looked pretty rock.

These cool snaps from Aimee Dars Ellis captured her life as a teenager with friends in the late 1980s. Most of photos were taken in Oklahoma, a few were from Texas.
