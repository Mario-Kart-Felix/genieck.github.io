About Us:
The Crafts Council of Karnataka's (CCK) is dedicated to helping the Indian crafts persons sustain their art by actively engaging with them and providing them with support in terms of product development and retailing. Our aim is not just to preserve the rich heritage of Indian handicrafts and hand looms, but also to revive dying crafts traditions by working with talented and dedicated designers who develop new interpretations so that they become relevant and self-sustaining in modern society. It is our intention to work directly with the crafts people and provide them with a forum where they can seek assistance along the entire process from learning the required skills, to product development and retailing. We are a NGO and work with the Govt. of India and the Directorate of Handicrafts towards achieving our goals.
