What are we?

The Marks Trading Company is small business focused on food.  We are concerned with where our food comes from and what has been added to our food. We are not food experts. We want to provide products that are local and regional when possible. We want to offer the freshest and least processed food available. We want “More Farm and Less Factory” in our foods.  We want to offer better food choices and help all of our customers make better meals for themselves and their families.

We currently offer hand blended spices for sale at local farmers markets and vendor shows.  We hope to open a specialty grocery store in downtown Adrian within the next 12-15 months. You can read more about us and our concept here.

Our Community Commitment

 

Being part of the local community has always been an important part of our project. Early on we knew we wanted to be in downtown Adrian.  From sourcing as many products as we can locally and regionally, to finding a location for our brick and mortar store, our community is key.

We plan on being more than a grocery store. We plan on offering educational classes such as, meal prep, how to classes, and general cooking demonstrations. We will work with local farmers to bring there produce and vegetables to the store as well as meat, eggs, and dairy.  Knowing the source of your food is just as important as knowing the family that has grown or raised it.
