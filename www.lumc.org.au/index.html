La Trobe Uni Mountaineering Club

** Please note LUMC's  outdoor activities are currently on hold due to COVID19. We are still active on Social media, like our Facebook page to stay involved, or if you can join as a member to support the club and be involved when we resume activities**

We are a Melbourne outdoor adventure club based at La Trobe University, Bundoora. Our activities are run at a range of levels catering from first time through to advanced.

Join us for a social and fun way to enjoy the outdoors and develop skills.

Bushwalking

Kayaking and Rafting

Rockclimbing

XC Skiing and Snowshoeing

Mountain Biking

Caving

 

Check out our calendar for upcoming trips

All members are encouraged to organise club events
