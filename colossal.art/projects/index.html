Inflatable Exhibition at the Exploratorium Museum

Seeking to bring in a larger audience and expand their reach to art-savvy adults, San Francisco’s #1 museum engaged Colossal to curate their summer 2018 exhibition. Starting with three fully-fledged exhibition concept proposals, Colossal worked closely with the museum’s leadership to select and shape the vision for an exhibition that would celebrate the connection between art and science, resonate with all ages, and appeal to an art-focused audience.

SoulCycle Art in Residence

Preeminent luxury wellness and exercise brand SoulCycle selected Colossal to develop a program of “art in residence” at SoulCycle flagship locations. To accent the regional beauty and surreal character of Las Vegas at the brand’s new location at Wynn Plaza in Las Vegas, opening fall 2018, Colossal tapped muralist Brendan Monroe. Monroe, an in-demand muralist based in Los Angeles, has also created custom on-site work for Twitter, Airbnb, and Equinox fitness.
