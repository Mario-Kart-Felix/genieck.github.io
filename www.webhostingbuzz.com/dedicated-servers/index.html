Dedicated Servers from WebHostingBuzz

All WebHostingBuzz dedicated servers come with top quality specifications and generous bandwidth quotas, all for a single monthly cost. Our servers benefit from free setup, and are deployed within hours. As an added benefit, all WebHostingBuzz dedicated server customers can take advantage of our free transfer service, meaning we’ll move all your existing sites from your current web host to a brand new server with us – for free!

At WebHostingBuzz, we pride ourselves on service & support. Our servers are securely housed in state-of-the-art data centers which are manned 24/7. For added peace of mind, we offer a 100% network uptime guarantee and help is never too far away with our in-house 24/7/365 technical support team.

The benefits of using a dedicated server means you won’t be sharing resources within anyone else; you’ll be the only client hosted on the server. So your CPU, hard disks and RAM will be 100% yours. We highly recommend utilizing the power of cPanel & WHM (WebHost Manager) control panel software for your server, where you’ll be able to manage every aspect of your dedicated server all from within a friendly and intuitive user interface. Our dedicated servers can handle high volumes of traffic with a dedicated 100Mbps data connection (upgradable to 1Gbps).

Same-Day Server Setup

From the moment you place your order, our technical team will begin provisioning your server hardware. We’ll then begin the process of installing your Operating System, configuring add-ons such as cPanel/WHM, and securing your server. We aim to provide a fully configured server within 4 hours.

24/7/365 Expert Support

Our experienced technical team are available to assist with any issues you may have 24/7, 365 days a year. Simply create a support ticket and we’ll do our best to respond as soon as possible with a resolution.

cPanel & WHM Control Panel

We offer cPanel & WHM control panel as a $25/mo add-on across our range of dedicated servers. Our team have many years experience supporting this platform and we’d recommend it if you’d like a straightforward way to manage multiple websites.

Do you replace any hardware which might fail?

Yes. We keep a local stock of replacement parts in our data centers. In case of a hardware failure our qualified specialists will replace any defective part promptly. We also monitor all our servers to be able to predict possible hardware failures and prevent data loss.
