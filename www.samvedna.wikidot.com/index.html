Sowing the seeds of harmony and knowledge - through Information and Communication Technology

Samvedna connects school children from across cities through Information and Communication Technology (ICT). Voice and data technology linked Him Jyoti School, Dehra Dun and Manava Bharati India International School, N Delhi to start with. This pilot programme was delivered by REACHA, a Voluntary Organisation, from October 2008 to September 2009 in partnership with TTSL. The programme has now become self sustaining, and more schools are being welcomed to join the Samvedna family by using their own internet connections. Students, teachers and parents can participate at their own pace in various Samvedna activities and Projects.

REACHA has conceived and designed this site. It is constantly updating it as per the evolving understanding of the target children, teachers and parents.

CBSE Circular No. 57 dated 1 September 2010 emphasises on the need to integrate ICT tool-kits in classroom teaching learning. Samvedna does just that…and much more!! Browse around to find out for yourself.
