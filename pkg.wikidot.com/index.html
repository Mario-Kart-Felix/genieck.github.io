In Soviet Greece, buttons mash YOU!

DISCLAIMER: This is not an english language wiki. It started out as a way to archive VF-realted discussions and present it in a centralised fashion to new VF players from greece. Hence most of the original content is in greek. That was more than 3 years ago.

Unfortunately nowadays greek fighting games fans have moved on from VF5 and there is no greek VF community to speak of. This has happened in the past too, while transitioning from VF4evo to VF5. One can only hope that, once a new VF version will come out, greek VF players will flock backl to the game.

Ena wiki gia VF combos, floats, stratigiki klp. Arxise me elaxisto periexomeno gia to VF4evo kai auto kurios san template. Ystera bgike to VF5 i floga tou VF 3ananapse - prosorina :-(

Tespa, pos mporeite na kanete pros8ikes?

Polu apla, sto kato meros ka8e selidas uparxoun diafora koumpakia. I pio amesi lusi einai na patisete to koumpi edit kai na pros8esete o,ti pisteuete pos leipei. Mia pio emesi lusi einai na patisete to discuss me to opoio mporeitai na kanete ena sxolio sxetika me tin selida. Parapano plirofories edo.

Ean den einai megalos kopos xrisimopoieite standard notation please kai oxi numpad . Nomizo pos auto to link apo to shoruyken e3igei ikanopoihtika giati. Pera apo auto, pros8este eleu8era, den xreiazeste kan na eiste logged in :-)
