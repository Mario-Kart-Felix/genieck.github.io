Become a Colossal Member

Support independent publishing and help us keep art accessible for everyone.

NOTE: As a result of the coronavirus pandemic, we are now publishing from our homes—in March we even moved our office into storage. The near-collapse of advertising on Colossal means drastic cuts, and we’re doing everything we can to keep the lights on (in our apartments) and the art and culture flowing to your inbox, our website, and social media channels. Membership is now our main source of revenue.

Without you, there is no us. Even if you can’t support us directly during this extraordinary time, it means the world to us to know you’re out there. If membership isn’t for you, consider a one-time donation via PayPal or by credit card through Donorbox.

Colossal is an international platform for contemporary art and visual expression that explores a vast range of creative disciplines. Since launching in 2010 we have published over 6,000 articles written by seven contributors, and pride ourselves in celebrating the work of both emerging and established artists through an ongoing commitment to make art accessible to everyone. Colossal sparks real-world change from grade-school art classrooms to some of the world’s leading museums and commercial galleries.

Christopher Jobson, Founder & Editor-in-Chief

Membership Perks

All membership levels include the following benefits & perks.

  • Ad-free Reading Experience
    Active members will not see banner advertising on Colossal when logged into their account.
  • Exclusive Interviews
    Introducing our exclusive bi-weekly artist interview series. Colossal interviews some of the most exciting, engaging, and brilliant contemporary artists, designers, makers, and creators from around the world. Recent interviews include Ai Weiwei, Icy & Sot, Bisa Butler, and many more.
  • Special Offers & Deep Discounts From Our Friends & Partners
    Enjoy discounts plus a rotating slate of savings and special offers from select friends. Currently: 15% off eligible prints and photos (and all custom framing) at 20×200, 15% off shirts at Super Superficial, 20% off Create! Magazine, and 10% off at the Booooooom Shop.
  • Members-only Newsletter
    A subscription to our monthly Membership News that includes details on upcoming features and events, news, and special perks.
  • Early Access & Discounts to Events
    Early invites + access to Colossal events, plus ticket discounts when possible.
  • 1% for Art in the Classroom Coronavirus Relief: Keep Kids Learning
    Colossal believes strongly in supporting art education. One percent of membership fees are allocated to DonorsChoose.org where we have purchased supplies and materials impacting 2,500 students to date. Note: We are temporarily diverting our donations to the Coronavirus Relief: Keep Kids Learning campaign.
  • The Colossal Grant
    Once we reach critical mass, Colossal will officially launch an annual grant fund that members can apply for. Stay tuned in 2020.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Colossal have a Membership program?
In order to establish and nurture a sustainable business that would allow us to keep moving forward in our work uplifting artists around the world, we diversified Colossal on Day One with a range of revenue streams. Digital advertising, e-commerce, and curatorial commissions all help us meet our bottom line, pay our staff, and keep our twinkling string of cafe lights on. As we look toward our tenth year and approach our seven thousandth (!) article published, we are proud to offer a new way for readers to help support the publication you know and love.
What do Memberships go toward?
Membership fees will be specifically earmarked toward staff salaries, site upkeep and hosting costs, rent, and other expenses relating directly to our day-to-day publication. In the near future, a portion of collective membership fees may also be allocated toward the Membership Grant program.
What if I'm not interested in the membership benefits? Can I still support Colossal with a one-time donation?
Yes, of course. You can always donate via PayPal or by credit card through Donorbox.
Are you putting Colossal behind a paywall?
Colossal will remain free and open to everyone, including all of our 15-20 weekly blog posts, email newsletters, and social media posts. However, Colossal Members gain access to our bi-weekly interview series and our new monthly Membership newsletter.
Do you have student/educator pricing?
Yes. Please contact us at [email protected] so we can validate your student or educator status and get you access to student pricing.
Do you offer discounts to teams?
Yes. Please contact us at [email protected] so we can review your request and find the appropriate pricing for your university, agency, or other group.
Can I give a Membership as a gift?
Yes! You can give our annual membership plan as a gift by clicking here.
Who manages the Membership and payments?
All membership charges are handled by Memberful with Stripe as a payment processor. All aspects of your membership benefits are handled by Colossal via email. Memberful may contact you with invoices, renewal notices, or expired credit cards alerts.
